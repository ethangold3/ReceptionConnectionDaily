How to Play Reception Connection Daily

Connect the start player to the end player through NFL passing connections.

  1. Start with the given player.
  2. Enter the name of a player who has caught a pass from or thrown a pass to the current player.
  3. Continue until you reach the end player.
  4. Come back tomorrow for a new pairing of players.

Example: If the starting player is Tom Brady and the destination player is Peyton Manning, then you can connect them via Wes Welker to create the path:

Tom Brady → Wes Welker → Peyton Manning.

Incorrect guesses send you in a random direction, signaled by a tornado. You will always be able to connect to the destination player, but you may be sent in a random direction.
